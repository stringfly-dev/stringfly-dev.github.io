PROJECT OVERVIEW

Coindee is a cryptocurrency platform designed to help users navigate the complexity of crypto investing through market insights, portfolio tools, and advisory services. The product serves both individual investors (B2C) and advisory clients (B2B).

THE CHALLENGE

The crypto market is highly volatile and difficult to understand, especially for new investors. Many users lack clear investment strategies and risk awareness, which often leads to poor decisions. The challenge was to design experiences that make information clearer, more actionable, and easier to trust.

COINDEE APP

Research

I conducted UX research using the Jobs To Be Done framework to understand investor goals and pain points. This included defining user journeys and analyzing competitor products in the crypto and financial space to identify gaps and opportunities for improvement.


App UI Improvements

Based on research insights, I redesigned key parts of the Coindee app, including the deposit flow, to reduce friction and improve clarity during critical user actions.

App store visuals

Designed a screenshot in the App Store for the Coindee Application.

COINDDEE ADVISORY (B2B & B2C)

Userflow

I collaborated with the advisory and marketing teams to define target users for both B2B and B2C services. We separated the advisory experience into clear flows, each structured around six key steps to improve usability and scalability.

Wireframes

After aligning on flow structure, I worked on low-fidelity wireframes. I was responsible for designing the report page, report history, and report templates for the advisory service.

Report Template

I designed reusable report templates for weekly and monthly advisory reports. These reports include market analysis, trend overviews, key news, and popular coin rankings making complex data easier to digest for users.

UI Design

I finalized the UI design for the advisory platform, ensuring consistency, readability, and a professional tone aligned with financial products.

KEY LEARNINGS & REFLECTIONS

  • This project strengthened my ability to design for complex financial products. I learned how to balance data density with clarity, collaborate across teams, and create scalable templates that support both product and business needs.
