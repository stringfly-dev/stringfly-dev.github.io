zTerminal

PROJECT OVERVIEW

zTerminal is an AI-powered crypto intelligence terminal designed to help traders discover insights faster, analyze market trends, and navigate complex data with clarity.

THE CHALLENGE

The interface was overwhelming with too much information and inconsistent visuals, making it hard for traders to get clear insights. My goal was to bring clarity, usability, and speed to the overall experience.

DESIGN APPROACH

Building the Design System

I built a scalable design system from scratch, covering tokens, typography, spacing, grids, components, and interaction patterns, helping developers and designers work faster and more consistently.

Visual Identity & Animation

I designed the zAI orb as the core visual symbol of the product, representing intelligence, energy, and AI-driven analysis, with animations for loading, transitions, and empty states to create a seamless experience.

Core AI Chat Experience

I designed the core AI chat experience with a clear interface, smooth loading states, and data widgets like price charts and trader cards, along with command actions that help users find information more quickly.

Homepage Customization

The old homepage was overwhelming, so I redesigned it into a customizable layout that allows users to reorder, hide, or prioritize sections, giving them full control over their homepage.

Smart Search & Command Input

I designed a smart search, command, mentions, and AI queries into one place. It helps users find information faster, discover commands more easily, and interact with zAI without switching contexts.

Trader Insight Profile

I redesigned the user profile to provide actionable insights about crypto creators and traders. The new profile highlights activity patterns, top mentioned coins, sentiment trends, and performance signals.

OUTCOMES & FEEDBACK

  • The internal team including developers and designers can work much faster by using the design system I created.
  • The design structure, consistency, and variables allowed teams to apply it seamlessly across different features and other projects.
  • The AI response widgets made the output more engaging by adding visualizations that help users understand information more easily.

KEY LEARNINGS & REFLECTIONS

  • Gained deeper experience in AI-focused design, especially in creating AI responses that strengthened my understanding across technology, data, and UX.
  • Learned how to build a comprehensive, scalable design system that supports the entire team effectively.
  • Collaborating closely with the PO and engineers helped me understand crypto and AI products more clearly, while competitor research improved my ability to evaluate strengths and weaknesses for better product decisions.
