PROJECT OVERVIEW

Nexus is a community-driven product that turns user activity on X (Twitter) into gamified missions and rewards. Users participate in missions across different communities, complete actions based on defined conditions, and earn rewards through engagement.

THE CHALLENGE

The main challenge was designing a system that connects social activity, gamification mechanics, and rewards in a way that feels clear and motivating. The product needed to handle complex mechanics missions, rankings, rewards, and partner integrations without overwhelming users.

DESIGN APPROACH

Community Feed

I designed the Community Feed to surface all key information in one place including community details, active missions, user rankings, and a feed of top posts from X related to each mission. This layout helps users understand what‚Äôs happening in a community and how they can participate at a glance.


Nexus integrates multiple partners to offer diverse missions and rewards tailored to user interests.
I designed the Communities page to support exploration through clear labels, active mission highlights, and a Rules & Rewards modal that explains how each mission works and what users can earn.

Design system & Partner Variables

Behind the partner integration, I built the Nexus design system and set up Figma variables to support partner branding. When a new partner joins, their color palette and visual assets can be applied quickly across design, development and marketing keeping the system consistent and scalable.

Guidebook

I designed the Guidebook to onboard users into Nexus and explain how the platform works. It introduces key concepts such as Watcher Power and walks users through the flow of connecting a wallet to participate in activities, become a Watcher, and start earning rewards.

Dashboard

The Dashboard is a personalized space where users can track progress, view activity, and manage their Nexus experience. One challenge was handling the complexity of the system tracking X posts, zap eligibility, wallet connections, and partner specific points.

I designed the dashboard to clearly surface user activity and allow users to dive into each community for transparency and clarity.


In addition, I designed the referral system, introducing a space and star theme concept, along with the
invite friend flow. The experience was designed responsively across desktop, tablet, and mobile.

Achievement

I was responsible for the Achievement feature, where users unlock achievement cards by completing missions on Nexus. I designed the UX/UI across default states, unlock screens, and collection views. I also created star animations on the cards to visually communicate rarity, along with unlock animations to enhance the reward moment.
