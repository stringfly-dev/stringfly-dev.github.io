PROJECT OVERVIEW

AISEKAI is an AI-powered entertainment platform that blends advanced language models with interactive storytelling and creator-driven content. The platform allows users to create, explore, and interact with AI characters‚Äîtransforming passive content consumption into immersive, two-way experiences.

THE CHALLENGE

Most digital content platforms are one-directional.
users consume content but have little influence over how it evolves. Creating content often requires high creative effort and technical knowledge, making it difficult for everyday users to become creators. At the same time, many platforms struggle to balance creative freedom with moderation.

DESIGN EXECUTION

Building a Scalable Foundation

I designed AISEKAI's visual identity from scratch, inspired by the isekai genre. The slime character was created as the brand mascot, representing playfulness and adaptability. I also built a scalable design system covering colors, typography, components, and branding guidelines to ensure consistency across the product.

Motion Design & Visual Communication

I edited a feature showcase video to present key product features and flows. In addition, I designed a slime loading animation used in the platform to provide visual feedback and add personality to loading states.

DESIGN INTERACTIVE EXPERIENCES

AI Conversation Experience

I designed the core chat interface where users interact with AI characters. Inspired by familiar interaction patterns such as Instagram Stories, the interface encourages exploration while keeping conversations natural and immersive.

MAKING CREATION ACCESSIBLE

Guided Character Creation

Creating AI characters was initially complex for non-technical users. I designed a guided creation flow that breaks the process into clear steps personality, prompts, scenarios, and roles.
By collaborating closely with the PO, PM, and engineers, I translated complex AI logic into simple UI patterns and reusable templates.

Impactüî•

(Top tags: Anime, OC, Fiction, Game)
SUPPORTING THE CREATOR ECOSYSTEM

Creator Profiles

I designed creator profiles to surface performance metrics, engagement signals, and social indicators. This helps users discover creators while giving creators visibility into how their characters perform within the community.

DRIVING PARTICIPATION & DISCOVERY

Themed Creation Events

To drive high-quality content and participation, I designed themed creation events that invite users to build characters around specific concepts.
Each event includes achievement badges to motivate engagement. I designed all badge visuals, including slime characters, rarity tiers (Bronze, Silver, Gold), and campaign assets.

Impactüî•
1,112 characters submitted through events
MONETIZATION WITHOUT BREAKING THE EXPERIENCE

Marketplace Experience

The Marketplace was designed to support monetization while preserving user experience. Users can purchase chat stickers, background themes, and priority server access.
I designed the credit flow, marketplace layout, priority pass system, and the first set of sticker visuals, along with promotional assets.
